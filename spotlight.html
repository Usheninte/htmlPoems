PANKY3S - SOIREE



"SOIREE" is a song by Afrobeat indie sensation @panky3s. He is a true artist with his craft. Soiree is an infusion of poetry, sensual rhythm and siren - like sounds. It is a track also included in his latest body of work - 1712L.


JABBS produced the track with expertise, and precision. No detail was overlooked in the production of this piece of art. The interplay between the words 'confession' and 'dancer', in the song, give the tune an overall surreal feel.


Enough said.
